13 Classic Makeup Looks You Can Wear Forever

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  695 words  |  Martina Birk

Makeup is nothing if not a vehicle for creativity. And though many of the modern world’s most viral makeup moments are saved and reimagined for their out-of-box appeal, there’s something to be said for a classic makeup look. But what makes a look “classic,” anyway? It all comes down to details.

Whether the moment references a timeless era, relies on elegant neutrals, or incorporates soft hues and subtle shimmer, classic beauty is simply pretty. Understated choices and juxtaposition are used to quietly demand attention—think red lips and a cat eye, nudes and bronzes, or extra-long lashes and brushed-up brows. 

From Zendaya’s fresh-faced beauty to Hailey Bieber’s no-makeup makeup, these classic makeup looks are forever. 

Gamine and Feline

A feline flick of liner and a red lip are an effective pairing, and Zoë Kravitz’s freckles and brushed-up brows lend an effortless feel to the moment. 

Pop of Color

Classic need not denote a lack of playful color. Gemma Chan’s more timeless details—dewy skin and eyes edged in jet-black mascara—are set off by a pop of coral swiped over lips and a subtle daub of the same color warming cheeks. 

Strong Neutrals

Glamorous neutrals never go out of style. For a barely-there makeup moment strong enough to steal shine on the red carpet, look to Hailey Bieber’s strong brows and taupey pink lip. A coat of mascara on the top lashes keeps things occasion-ready, as does a quietly carving pull of berry blush. 

Noir Glamour

Vamp is classic, too. This throwback moment from Rihanna offers a lesson in contemporary noir beauty, matte merlot lips paired with glowy skin, gilded lids, and feathered lashes. Shapely brows and a pull of liquid liner along the upper lashline further the moment’s inherent impact—it all comes down to a retro romance.

Latte Makeup

Latte makeup, meet Jennifer Lopez. The pop star generally prefers a sumptuous neutral, and this cocktail of nude, bronze, and barely-there shimmer rings eternal—particularly when finished with extra-long lashes. 

Touch of Shimmer

For those who favor a bit of shimmer, Gigi Hadid’s take on classic is all down to subtle shine. Warming apricot blush and a peachy pink lip team with a dusting of glitter around the eyes make for an angelic beauty look. 

Warm and Neutral

An assemblage of warming notes comprises Priyanka Chopra’s luminous look. Bold brows and lash-heavy lids pressed in bronze shimmer play well with a peony pout, the moment neutral enough for seasonless wear. 

Mod Moment

The 1960s serve as inspiration for Margot Robbie’s enduring moment. Modish touches like graphic liner, pale shadow, and peachy lips and cheeks are all appealingly retro but still entirely relevant. 

Glass Lips and Cat Eyes

Janelle Monáe’s classic glamour is all about depth and shine. Radiant skin and glassy lips brighten, while black-rimmed eyes, bold brows, and exaggerated feathered lashes deliver the drama. The resulting look feels fresh, subtly striking, and at home in any era.

Classic Red Lip

Taylor Swift’s trademark take on classic makeup comes down to two details: cat-eye liner and a bright red lip. The pop star rarely strays from the tried-and-true combination, and only occasionally iterates on the classic crimson hue. 

Cool Shimmer and Berry Lips

A berried lip and silver shadow mark this classic makeup moment from Marion Cotillard. The cool application is a study in French girl beauty, sophisticated glamour balanced by a sense of fun. The diffusion of shimmering shadow begins at the inner corner of each eye, giving way to subtly smoked lids and framed by soft, generous lashes. And as for lips a cheeks? A daub of strawberry toes the line between warm and cool. 

True Romance

Zendaya can carry off any (and we mean any) beauty look. So when the actress goes classic, her interpretation is worthy of analysis. Brushed-up brows, extra-long lashes, and a glossy pink lip play off a rumpled updo to create an enterally romantic aesthetic. 

Sultry Pinks

Classic makeup centers on a balance of bold and soft detailing, as demonstrated by Lily-Rose Depp. A tight-lined gaze offers an easy edge made a touch gentler through pale pink shadow and a nude lip.

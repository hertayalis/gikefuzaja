The Bold and the Beautiful Preview: Week of November 13 Eric Refuses Familys Sympathy, Demand

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  533 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers for the week of November 13 reveals a new preview video with heart-wrenching scenes. Eric Forrester (John McCook) declares that he doesn’t live his life with pity, and won’t die with it.

Meanwhile, several members of the Forrester and Logan families learn about Eric’s grim fate. So, let’s get started discussing what is coming up in the CBS soap opera.

Brooke Logan Forrester’s Bombshell

B&B spoilers for the week of November 13 reveal that the preview video shows Brooke trying to deliver some tough news to her daughter. Brooke tells Hope Logan (Annika Noelle) that Eric is dying.

Hope will be completely shocked by this news. Hope might do the same thing as Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) and ask about alternative treatments. ‘

Hope might be even more shocked by what Brooke says next. Since Eric doesn’t want anyone to know he is dying, Brooke will likely tell Hope to keep it to herself.

Of course, Thomas Forrester (Matthew Atkinson) will soon find out as well. That will provide some opportunities for “Thope” to bond on a deeper level.

B&B Spoilers: Steffy’s Reaction

Speaking of Thomas, both he and Steffy Forrester Finnegan (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) will learn the truth during the week of November 13.

Ridge had a chance to tell Steffy about Eric when she came back from Paris. However, Steffy already has her hands full, so Ridge didn’t think it was the right time.

The preview video clip shows Brooke standing by Ridge’s side as he tells his children the truth. Both Steffy and Thomas are stunned to learn that Eric only has a few months to live.

Immediately, Steffy announces that she needs to see Eric. That must be when Ridge tells Steffy and Thomas that Eric doesn’t want anyone to know about his diagnosis.

Donna Logan’s Frustration

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers for the week of November 13 reveal that the preview video shows Eric continuing to make declarations.

Eric will announce once again that he doesn’t want anyone’s pity. A very frustrated and agitated Eric says he doesn’t live his life with pity and certainly won’t die that way, either.

After Eric’s powerful but heartbreaking declaration, Katie simply looks down at her feet without saying a word.

However, Donna’s face implies she is getting fed up with Eric’s tough guy stance.

The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Eric Forrester’s Way

Eric got his way with the fashion showdown and now is sitting at the throne of Forrester Creations, just as he wished.

So, with Eric continuing to insist that he will live and die his way, what exactly does that mean?

Fans will also see some scenes of Katie tearfully hugging Eric. Plus, Ridge tells Brooke that he doesn’t want to lose his dad. So, expect a lot of tear-jerking scenes.

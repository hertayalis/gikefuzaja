Jennifer Lopez's Leg Slit Moment in Custom Michael Costello Gown

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill
  • Jennifer Lopez just hit the red carpet in Manhattan wearing a sparkly gown with a major leg slit.
  • But it wasn't for a real-life event, rather Lopez was filming her new movie, Marry Me, alongside Owen Wilson.
  • The Hustlers star was photographed on set wearing an extravagant wedding gown.

Jennifer Lopez might be engaged to Alex Rodriguez, but she was photographed wearing a showstopping silver gown and posing alongside actor Owen Wilson just last night in New York City.

Fans shouldn't worry though, as Lopez is definitely still engaged to Rodriguez. The actress and singer is currently filming her new movie, Marry Me, in Manhattan, and her latest scene involved hitting the red carpet with Wilson.

J.Lo wore a custom Michael Costello gown, which was covered in silver sequins and featured a revealing leg slit. The one-sleeved, caped gown was simply jaw-dropping on Lopez, who was photographed holding onto Wilson's arm during filming.

We're guessing the "random guy" is Wilson, and it's impossible to deny that they make a cute couple on the red carpet.

The Bachelor Alum Amanda Stanton is Married to Fiance Michael Fogel

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  482 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Source: IG

  • The relationship status of ‘The Bachelor’ alum Amanda Stanton is now married
  • She recently walked down the aisle with the real estate entrepreneur Michael Fogel
  • The two were friends for two years before dating and made their relationship public in February 2021

Amanda Stanton, 32, and her fiance Michael Fogel are officially married!

‘The Bachelor’ alum is now no more a bachelorette. Amanda and Michael wed in a ‘timeless’ intimate ceremony in California on Friday, September 2.

Similarly, the ceremony was held at Kestrel Park in Santa Ynez, California, which is in Santa Barbara County about 120 miles north of Los Angeles.

The reality star remarked,

“It’s so special to make this commitment. I’m so excited and happy.I wanted everything to be very timeless,’ Stanton said, adding, It’s an elevated modern garden wedding, very simple.”

Also, she posted an IG story ahead of the wedding that showed her being driven dressed in her Monique Lhuillier gown and wrote “On my way to MY wedding! freaking out” on side of her IG story.

The newly married couple were friends for two years before dating. In February 2021, the two made their relationship announcement on social media.

The real estate entrepreneur and the reality star got engaged this past December.

Wedding Details

The bride wore a Monique Lhuillier gown with long sleeves for her special day. Michael, on the other hand, dressed down in a matching white tuxedo with a black bow tie, pants, and shoes.

Along with their close friends and family, fellow Bachelor Nation alums Lauren Bushnell Lane, Andi Dorfman, Emily Ferguson, Raven, and Adam Gottschalk also attended for their big day.

Stanton’s oldest daughter, whom she shared with her ex-husband, played a special role during the ceremony. She had the honor of being her mother’s maid of honor while her younger sister served as the flower girl.

Amanda has two daughters Kinsley, 10, and Charlie, 8 who she co-parents with ex-husband Nick Buonfiglio.

The 32-years-old reality star mentioned that her both daughters are very happy with their positions.

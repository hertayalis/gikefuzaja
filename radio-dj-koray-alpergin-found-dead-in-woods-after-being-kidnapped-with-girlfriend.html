Tragic Discovery of Radio DJ Koray Alpergins Body in Woods Prompts Grief and Shock

 Posted on June 11, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  206 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Reports surfaced over the weekend revealing the tragic discovery of a prominent radio DJ’s body in the woods of Essex, UK.

The deceased, Koray Alpergin, aged 43 and known for owning the London station Bizim FM, was found by Essex Police near Loughton on Saturday, October 15, in response to a missing person report involving a man and a woman.

As a development, three suspects were apprehended in the Kent area on suspicion of murder and continue to be held in custody by authorities.

It was reported by Turkish media that the couple had been abducted from their residence.

An outpouring of tributes to the popular DJ has been witnessed.

One individual, Altan Akturk, shared on social media: “My dear friends from London on my page; My friend and colleague dear Koray and his girlfriend, who are well known to the public in London, have unfortunately been missing since Thursday.”

Another friend, known on Facebook as Bay HIC, expressed: “The description of the death of my friend and colleague, dear Koray, whom the society in London knows very well, is an impossible pain and sadness… I wish condolences and patience to all those who love him, especially his family. May Allah have mercy upon them. Amen.”

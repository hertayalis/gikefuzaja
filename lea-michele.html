Glee Actress, Lea Michele and Her Husband Of Three Years, Zandy Reich Welcomed a Son in 2020

Lea Michele is known as one of the strong American actress, singer, and is the NY Times Best Selling author in the entertainment industry. She gained international recognition after playing the main role of Rachel Berry in the series Glee (2009–2015) on Fox.

More to that, she is known for her roles including as Valentina Barella on ABC The Mayor in 2017 and as Hester Ulrich in Scream Queens (2015–2016) on Fox. Stick with us to find more about her.

Who is Lea Michele Married To?

The 34 years old Lea Michele is happily married to her long term husband, Zandy Reich. He is a businessman who serves for the AYR clothing company as the president since 2014.

They started dating in July 2017. The entrepreneur Zandy, 37, popped the question to his girlfriend, Lea in April 2018 with a 4-carat diamond ring. "They couldn't be happier," a source stated. Finally, they exchanged the vows on 8th March 2019 in Northern California after two years of dating. 

The newlyweds said Yes I do in the presence of nearly 200 guests including Emma Roberts. The bride wore a white strapless silk wedding Monique Lhuillier's gown and the groom wore blue casual suits. Likewise, their intimate nuptials were officiated by Glee and Scream Queens creator Ryan Murphy.

Past Love Affairs

Michele who made her film debut with New Year's Eve in 2011, has been in a few romantic relationships with notable personalities. She first linked up with actor Landon Beard from August 2006 to June 2008.

Then, she was rumoured to be in a relationship with Matthew Morrison after getting cosy at the Broadway theatre in 2007. However, they never confirm. For two years, she has dated John Lloyd Young, an actor and singer between August 2008 and April 2009.

Lea started dating Theo Stockman in October 2009 after first meeting at the Broadway theatre. However, they parted ways in 2011 after two years of dating.

Welcomed a Baby in 2020

The 1.59 m tall Spring Awakening alum and her husband of three years Reich are blessed with one little baby boy. Together they welcomed a first child, a son on August 20, 2020. And the happy parents named their child Ever Leo Reich.

The three Grammy Award nominations shared the black and white snap of newborn's tiny feet on her Instagram page. She wrote: "ForEver grateful for this true blessing,". However, she announced her pregnancy on 2nd May 2020 by sharing the baby bump photo on the IG page. 

How Much is Lea Michele Worth?

Lea Michele has an impressive net worth of $14 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. She has earned her worth from her successful acting, singing career and as well as sponsorships and brand partnerships.

For her best performance on the Fox television series Glee, Michele made $80,000 per episode. Her costars including Chris Colfer and Amber Riley has earned over $45,000. Along with Glee, she has worked in Scream Queens and The Mayor. Apart, she has released three albums named Louder, Places, and Christmas in the City. 

The Broadway veteran has done partnership with various brands like Chevrolet, Nike, Gap, Dove, ZOLA, L’Oréal Paris, etc. Along with this, she has authored two books named Brunette Ambition in 2014 and You First: Journal Your Way to Your Best Life in 2015.

Bio, Childhood and Early Life

Lea Michele was born on August 29, 1986, in The Bronx, New York, the United States as Lea Michele Sarfati. She is the single child of Mark David Sarfati, a real estate agent and Edith Thomasina, a nurse.

Michele studied the Rockland Country Day School, Tenafly High School, and Stagedoor Manor. Later she attended the Tisch School of the Arts at New York University. So far, Michele holds American citizenship and is of white ethnicity.

Megan Fox and Kaia Gerber Keep Wearing Vans Old Skool Sneakers

 Posted on June 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  371 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Contrary to style icon Carrie Bradshaw's opinion, we think you can have too many pairs of shoes — especially when you're buying $900 Manolo Blahniks on the regular. However, we do firmly believe there's one type of shoe you can reasonably rack up forever: sneakers. No matter what trendy footwear is currently blowing up your Instagram feed (we're looking at you, clogs and Crocs), sneakers will never lose their appeal. And the latest sneaker we're adding to our collection — thanks to Megan Fox's seal of approval — is the Vans Old Skool Sneaker.

This fall, celebrities have been stepping out in Vans sneakers left and right; supermodels Kaia Gerber and Emily Ratajkowski consistently hit the NYC pavement in them, Kristen Stewert often opts for the kicks while traveling, Olivia Wilde repeatedly rocks Vans checkered slip-ons while running errands, and just earlier this week, Megan Fox was spotted in Greece (predictably) hand-in-hand with Machine Gun Kelly while wearing the Vans Old Skool Sneaker.

Shop now: $60; nordstrom.com

Although cool-girl Megan wore the Vans Old Skool Sneakers with a comfy sweatsuit and ballcap, the ways to style them are endless. The sporty shoes pair perfectly with casual jeans and T-shirts, but as Gerber has proven on multiple occasions, the sneaker-sundress-combo is a staple summer style, too. Vans are fitting for countless outings: grocery store runs à la Rachel Bilson, coffee walks courtesy of Lucy Hale, or even bike rides in bikinis like Kourtney Kardashian.

Made with suede and exaggerated stitching, these versatile sneakers add a slight edge to any look any time of the year (unlike Ms. Bradshaw's fancy stilettos.) The signature stripe is instantly recognizable, and as the OG skater sneaker, no other brand does retro-cool kicks like Vans. Plus, the prices are reasonable — unlike other celeb-loved sneakers from brands like Golden Goose and Yeezy.
